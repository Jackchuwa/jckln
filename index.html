THE SEVENTH ANNUAL GENERAL MEATING OF CONVOCATION OF THE EASTERN AFRICA STATISTICAL TRAINING CENTRE

Overview of the seventh convocation of the Eastern Africa Stastical Training Centre

The seventh Convocation of the Eastern Africa Stastical Training Centre of the year 2024 continues the tradition within academic institutions of gathering together academicians, students, alumni, benefactors, and industries proffesionals as stakeholders of the university community. It is the time to exchange views on the topical issues that touch teaching, reserch, and proffesional development.

This year convocation topic has not been selected yet but as always it will be standing point to reflect on the theme of this year's 18 November African Statistical Day.

African Statistical Day is celebrated each year. The commemoration aims among others, to increase public awareness about the important role which statistics play in all aspects of social and economic development.
